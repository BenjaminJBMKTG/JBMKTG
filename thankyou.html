Questions You Might Have

What should I prepare for the call? +

Bring your current Google Ads performance (if you're running ads), your average order value, profit margins, and monthly revenue goals. The more context you give us, the better we can tailor our strategy to your business.

How much does it cost to work with you? +

What kind of results can I expect? +

It depends on your product, margins, and market. We're not going to promise 10x ROAS overnight. What we will do is build campaigns that are profitable from day one, then scale them aggressively. Most of our ecommerce clients see positive ROI within the first 60-90 days.

Do you work with brands in my niche? +

We work exclusively with ecommerce brands across all niches (fashion, home goods, health/beauty, electronics, etc.). If you sell physical products online, we can help. We'll discuss your specific niche and competition on the call.

What if Google Ads isn't right for my business? +

We'll tell you. Seriously. If your margins are too thin, your product isn't search-friendly, or your market is too small, we're not going to take your money. We'd rather be honest and point you in the right direction than waste your time and ours.

How involved do I need to be? +

Not very. We handle everything from strategy to execution. You'll get weekly updates and monthly deep-dive reports. We'll need your input on creative assets and landing pages, but day-to-day campaign management is all on us.
