Google Ads Structure for Ecom Stores

Full-Funnel Framework: Problem Aware → Solution Aware → Product Aware

Top of Funnel
Problem Aware
"I have a problem but don't know the solution"
25% Budget
Performance Max (Discovery) ~15%
  • YouTube placements
  • Display Network
  • Gmail ads
Dynamic Search Ads (DSA) ~10%
  • Auto-targets from website content
  • Catch long-tail searches
Example Searches
  • How to fix dry skin
  • Solutions for cold emails
  • Best way to track inventory
Middle of Funnel
Solution Aware
"I know the solution, exploring options"
50% Budget
Generic Search Campaigns ~25%
  • Ad Group: [Product Category] keywords
  • Ad Group: [Feature] keywords
  • Exact & phrase match
High Priority Shopping ~15%
  • All products feed
  • Broad discovery
Competitor Campaigns ~10%
  • [Competitor] alternative
  • vs [Competitor]
Example Searches
  • Best running shoes for marathon
  • CRM software for small business
  • Nike alternative trainers
Bottom of Funnel
Product Aware
"I know your brand, ready to buy"
35% Budget
Brand Search Campaigns ~10%
  • Exact Brand Name
  • Brand + Product
  • Misspellings
Medium Priority Shopping ~20%
  • Best sellers only
  • Higher bids
Low Priority Shopping (Brand) ~5%
  • Exact brand terms in Shopping
Performance Max (Retargeting) ~15%
  • Audience: Past visitors
  • Audience: Cart abandoners
  • Asset Group: Best sellers
Example Searches
  • [Your Brand] discount code
  • [Your Brand] blue light glasses
  • [Your Brand] vs [Competitor]
Key Principles

Start at the Bottom

Build from bottom of funnel upward. Protect brand terms and prove profitability before expanding.

Shopping = Discovery

Shopping campaigns are your product discovery engine. Feed optimization is critical.

Search = Intent Capture

Search campaigns capture active intent. Match keywords to funnel stage.

PMax Fills Gaps

Only add Performance Max once you have conversion data. Let Google find opportunities.

Budget Follows Performance

Allocate more budget to what's working. Review and adjust weekly.

Protect Your Brand

Always run brand campaigns. Don't let competitors steal your traffic.

Starter Account Focus (Bottom + Middle First)

  • Brand Search: 20%
  • Shopping (all priorities): 60%
  • Generic Search: 20%
  • Skip PMax/DSA until you have data

Scaling Account (Full Funnel Coverage)

  • Brand Search: 10%
  • Shopping (all priorities): 40%
  • Generic Search: 25%
  • Competitor: 10%
  • PMax: 15%
