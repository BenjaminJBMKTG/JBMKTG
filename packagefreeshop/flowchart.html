🎯 Strategic Recommendations

  • Start with exact and phrase match to control costs and qualify traffic during launch phase
  • Brand campaign should have 90%+ impression share — protect your brand terms
  • Segment by product category for better ad copy relevance and landing page matching
  • Performance Max complements Search — use negative keywords in Search to avoid overlap
  • Remarketing is critical for eco-conscious shoppers who research before buying
  • Focus on EWG Verified and zero waste USPs in ad copy — strong differentiators
  • Test responsive search ads with sustainability messaging vs. product-specific benefits
