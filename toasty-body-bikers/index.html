Common Questions

Will it fit under my motorcycle leathers?
Yes! Our heated layers are designed specifically to be ultra-slim and flexible. They're made from thin, technical fabric with low-profile heating elements that sit flat against your body. Thousands of bikers wear them under leather jackets, textile suits, and racing leathers with no issues.
How long does the battery actually last?
On low heat (25¬∞C), up to 10 hours. Medium (35¬∞C) gives you 6-7 hours, and high (45¬∞C) runs for 3-4 hours. Most riders use low or medium for commuting and save high for really cold days. You can also connect to your bike's USB port for unlimited runtime.
Is it safe to wear while riding?
Absolutely. All ToastyBody products are CE certified for electrical safety. The heating elements are sealed and waterproof, and the system has automatic shut-off if it overheats (which it won't). It's safer than piling on bulky layers that restrict your movement and visibility.
What if I need a different size or don't like it?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If it doesn't fit or you're not satisfied, just send it back for a full refund or exchange. We also have detailed sizing guides on each product page, and our customer support team can help you choose the right size.
Can I wash them?
Yes! Remove the battery pack, and you can machine wash on a gentle cycle. Hang to dry (don't tumble dry). The heating elements are fully sealed and waterproof, so washing won't damage them at all.
Do I need to buy a special battery?
Nope! Any standard USB power bank works (we recommend 10,000mAh or higher for all-day use). If you already have one for your phone, it'll work. You can also plug directly into your bike's USB port if you have one installed.
