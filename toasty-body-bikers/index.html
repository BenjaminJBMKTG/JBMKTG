Common Questions About The Heated Base Layer Set

Will it really fit under my motorcycle leathers?
Absolutely. The Heated Base Layer Set is designed specifically for bikers. It's ultra-thin with low-profile heating elements that sit flat against your body. Thousands of riders wear it under leather jackets, textile suits, and racing leathers with zero bulk or restriction. You'll forget you're wearing it until you feel the warmth.
How long does the battery actually last on a ride?
On low heat (25°C), you get up to 10 hours - perfect for all-day touring. Medium (35°C) gives 8 hours, and high (45°C) runs for 6 hours. Most riders use low or medium for commuting and save high for freezing mornings. You can also connect to your bike's USB port for unlimited runtime on long tours.
Is it safe to wear while riding? What about crashes?
Yes, completely safe. The Heated Base Layer Set is CE certified for electrical safety. The heating elements are sealed, waterproof, and flexible - they move with you. The system has automatic shut-off protection if it overheats (which it won't). It's actually safer than wearing bulky layers that restrict your movement and control of the bike.
What power bank do I need? Do I have to buy a special one?
Any standard USB power bank works. We recommend 10,000mAh or higher for all-day use. If you already have one for charging your phone, it'll work fine. The set comes with a USB cable - just plug in and go. You can also connect directly to your bike's USB port if you have one installed.
Can I wash the heated base layer set?
Yes! Remove the power bank, and you can machine wash on a gentle cycle. Hang to dry (don't tumble dry). The heating elements are fully sealed and waterproof - they're designed to handle rain, sweat, and washing without any issues. Easy care for hard-working gear.
